How to make pizza dough.

Simple and quick, yet pretty tasty pizza dough!

The dough turns out crunchy!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • A glass of water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Pour oil and yeast in a bowl and stir well
  2. Add water to the mixture and stir more
  3. Finally, add salt and flour. Don't add to much, it should be relatively sticky
